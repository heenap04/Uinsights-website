ABOUT CHITKARA UNIVERSITY

Established in 2002 by the Chitkara Educational Trust, Chitkara University emerged as a beacon of educational excellence in Punjab, India. Officially recognized by the Punjab State Legislature under “The Chitkara University Act” in 2010, the institution has been committed to providing high-quality education that is both globally recognized and industry-relevant.

Situated on a sprawling campus conveniently located between Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, Ambala, and Patiala, Chitkara University offers a diverse range of multidisciplinary programs. From business management to nursing, computer science to hotel management, the university provides a comprehensive educational experience that prepares students for success in their chosen fields.

At the heart of Chitkara University's ethos lies a dedication to academic excellence, fueled by state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern classrooms, and well-equipped laboratories. The university fosters a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities, ensuring holistic growth and development.

Chitkara University takes pride in its strong national and international collaborations, which not only enhance academic standards but also offer students valuable opportunities for global exposure. The campus boasts a vibrant multicultural community, with a significant number of international students contributing to the rich tapestry of diversity.

VISION & MISSION

VISION:

To be a globally recognized institution dedicated to advancing academic excellence through interdisciplinary research and innovation.

MISSION:

  • To uphold global standards of education through active engagement with teachers, students, and industry partners.
  • To foster a culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship in collaboration with leading industry and academic institutions worldwide.
  • To instill ethical values and professional integrity in our students, faculty, and staff, nurturing responsible citizens and leaders.
  • To contribute to the development of a skilled society through education and community engagement.

OUR VALUES

At Chitkara University, we uphold a set of core values that guide our actions and decisions:

  • Care: Prioritizing the welfare of our students and employees.
  • Support: Going above and beyond to support all stakeholders.
  • Academic Excellence: Striving for excellence in teaching, learning, and research.
  • Integrity: Conducting ourselves with honesty, transparency, and ethicality.
  • Diversity: Embracing and promoting diversity in all aspects of university life.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and embracing innovative solutions.
  • Collaboration: Building meaningful partnerships for collective growth and success.
  • Accountability: Taking responsibility for our actions and outcomes.
  • Social Responsibility: Promoting social mobility and access to education for all.

Join us at Chitkara University, where we empower individuals to explore their potential and make a positive impact on the world.
