ABOUT

Welcome to our campus, a vibrant hub of learning and innovation nestled Rajpura, Punjab. Our campus offers a dynamic and inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and thrive. With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a wide range of academic programs, we strive to provide our students with a holistic education that prepares them for success in their chosen fields. Whether you're interested in the arts, sciences, business, or technology, our campus has something for everyone. Join us and be a part of our community dedicated to excellence and achievement.

Chitkara University, Punjab has made secure and safe arrangement of transport facility for students, staff & faculty. The transport facility is available from Chandigarh, Mohali, Kharar, Patiala, Derabassi, Panchkula, Ambala, Kalka, Zirakpur, Landran, Rajpura, Banur, Khanna, Sirhind, Doraha and Ludhina.
